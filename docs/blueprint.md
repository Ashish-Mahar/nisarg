# **App Name**: Nisarg Sindhe

## Core Features:

- Hero Showcase: A full-screen section to introduce the dancer with background visuals (image/video), name, tagline, and a 'Watch My Work' call-to-action.
- About Section: Dedicated section detailing the dancer's background and core dance styles (e.g., Hip-hop, Bollywood, Contemporary).
- Portfolio Gallery: Displays embedded Instagram dance videos and placeholder images to showcase the dancer's work.
- Services Offered: Lists professional services such as wedding choreography, dance classes, and stage performances.
- Contact & Social Media: A contact section including a direct contact form for inquiries and a link to the dancer's Instagram profile.
- Responsive Layout: Ensures the entire website adapts seamlessly for optimal viewing across various devices, from mobile phones to laptops.
- Interactive Animations: Integrates smooth scrolling, subtle hover effects, and elegant transitions to enhance user experience and visual appeal.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant electric purple (#9947EB) for a dynamic and artistic energy, providing striking highlights against the dark theme.
- Background color: A rich, nearly black shade with a subtle purple tint (#0D0B0E) to create a sophisticated dark theme that emphasizes other elements.
- Accent color: A brilliant neon cyan (#6AE4FB) used for interactive elements and glowing effects, ensuring strong contrast and visual interest.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, bold, and slightly futuristic feel. Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for clean, highly legible content that maintains a professional aesthetic.
- Minimalist, sleek icons that adopt the 'neon' aesthetic, particularly for social media links and navigation, to maintain a professional yet dynamic look.
- One-page, full-screen section design with smooth vertical scrolling to create an immersive and continuous user journey. Content is center-aligned where appropriate to draw focus.
- Subtle yet engaging animations including smooth transitions between sections, glowing effects on interactive elements like buttons and links, and soft parallax for background visuals.
